About Me

Hi, my name is Christian Landaverde. I am an online and in person fitness coach and trainer.

I began my fitness journey during my teenage years in high school because I was really skinny. I got a gym membership at the age of 18 when I graduated high school, and as time went on I started to have a passion for intense weight training.

Since then I have always enjoyed the process of physically and mentally changing for the better. I believe everyone is capable of becoming the best version of themselves, so I want to spread inspiration and motivation to many, to show them that it is possible!

I did not have a trainer when I started, so I spent a lot of time watching YouTube videos. I have had many injuries in the process, so I have spent the last 5 years researching fitness, and better ways to work out and eat. I also took some nutrition, and fitness classes in College.

Let me help you save you all that time, and injuries by sharing my knowledge with you, so that you can start your fitness journey.
