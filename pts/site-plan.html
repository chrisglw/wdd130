Overview

Purpose

This is my personal training website for people who want to change their body, and reach its best potential to feel and look healthier. I will provide you with a personalized training program, and work 1-on-1 to help you transform your body, and become the best version of YOURSELF!

I will help you set personal goals, and provide personalized guidance based on your needs, time, and progress; so that you can reach your fitness goals.

Audience

Everybody around the world who have access to the internet and are willing to transform their body to reach its best potential with online programs created specifically for each individual. People who live in Rexburg, Idaho area are able book 1 on 1 in person sessions. We require to sign a waiver that we are not liable nor responsible for any injuries that may occur during the use of our programs; therefore, we are looking forward to work with people above 18 years old

Programs

Online

My online program is designed so that you can work out from anywhere in the world whether you decide to work at home or at the gym.

Once you sign up, I am going to schedule a zoom call with you, so I can design your fully customized workout and nutrition plan.

Go ahead and sign up now, so that we can start with your transformation to become the best version of yourself!

In Person

Have you thought about going to the gym, but you feel intimidated because you do not really know what you are doing?

Do not worry! You can schedule an in person session with me, and I will be next to you during the whole workout session. I will be personally showing you how to execute the exercises step by step, counting your repetitions, and checking the rest time.

I'll be in touch with you within 2 - 3 business days to schedule an 1 on 1 in person training session.
